Extra information

Payment at an A.S.Adventure store

If you pay at the checkout of your A.S.Adventure store, you can use your voucher over multiple visits. You 'll be given a new voucher for the balance remaining.

Using as payment at our webshop

When you use your voucher at the webshop, you must use the full amount in one transaction. Don't forget to fill in the barcode and webcode!

Validity of your gift voucher

All our gift vouchers can be used once only, and are valid for up to 12 months from the date of issue.
